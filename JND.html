HUMAN SUBJECT TESTING

PROBLEM DEFINITION

To implement wearables in our daily life applications, understanding human perception against the wearables should be preceded. Among other haptic sensors, we specifically targeted the two joint receptors (proprioceptors):

  • Muscle Spindle measures the length and speed change of the msucle fibers.
  • Golgi Tendon Organ detects the tension of the muscle fibers.

TEST SETUP

Therefore, following joint conditions are explored during the test:

  • when arm is fully constrained (muscles isometrically contracted) by external forces.
  • When arm is freely posed at neutral angle (90 degrees).
  • When arm is moving under the certain speed.
We measured the subjects' perceptual responses to the exoskeleton's torque during the above conditions.
